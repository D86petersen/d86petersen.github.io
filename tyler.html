Tyler, My First Bonus Child!

This is my Step son Tyler. He is soon to be 14 years old. He is on the Autism Spectrum. He is shy at times, but there are times that he can be very outgoing and funny.

Learn More

Autism Awareness

Very Intelligent

Despite having Autism, Tyler is a straight A student. He is very intelligent and learns new things easily. I am very proud of him.

Shy Guy

Tyler is a shy guy. He is very quiet and doesn't talk much. He is very observant and notices everything. He is very smart and knows how to get what he wants.

Jokester

Despite being shy, Tyler likes to joke around with the people that he feels comfortable with. There aren't many. I consider myself lucky that he feels comfortable around me.

Major Gamer

This kids life revolves around video games. He is a major gamer. He plays on his Playstation, his Nintendo Switch, and his PC. He also enjoys playing VR games. I really want to get him into programming. I Think he would really enjoy it.
