Administrative Assistant

I was once part of the Fresno Rescue Mission program. As part of the program we had to work a job to help pay for our stay. I was given the position of Administrative Assistant for the Fresno Rescue Mission. I worked there for about 9 months in that position before graduating the program and beginning my job as a Roofer. I was responsible for answering phones, filing, and other administrative duties.

While working as an Administrative Assistant I learned a lot of valuable skills. I learned how to manage my time, how to work with empathy for others, as well as learning how to be more organized. I also learned valuable communications skills that I was lacking before.
