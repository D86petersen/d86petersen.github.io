Roofing

When I was about to graduate from the Fresno Rescue Mission, my mentor told me that he owned a company and had a job for me. I started working as a roofer in 2018. He shortly after starting made me a supervisor.

I worked for this company for about 2 years. I learned a lot about roofing and how to work with a team. I also learned how to work with customers and how to be a leader. I was able to work with a lot of different people and I was able to learn a lot about myself. Sadly I had to leave this company because the owners of the company were closing the business down and moving out of state.
