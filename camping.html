Camping

Fun for the whole family

My Family and I love to go camping as well. We usually do a yearly camping trip with a couple of other families that are close friends of ours. It is always a blast. Nothing like being able to run around in nature and teach the kiddos how to make a fire, or how to fish, or even how to chop fire wood. The only thing that I don't like about camping is fighting with the tent. There have been a few good times of frustration due to my tent. Getting out into nature is very relaxing and fun. It can also be very tiring though. I would like to continue our family camping trips every year.
