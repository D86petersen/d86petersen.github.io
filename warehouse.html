Warehouse

Also during the pandemic, I had worked for a while in a feed store warehouse. This was the main feed store for the city of Del Mar, California. The track in Del Mar is popular for thoroughbred horse racing. I got to meet many interesting people during my time at this job.

This job was very labor intensive. There were some days that I would unload a whole semi-truck full of bails of hay by myself. Needless to say it reminded me how hard physical labor can be. Good thing I was already use to it from my roofing job.
