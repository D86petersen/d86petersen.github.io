Fishing

One of my most relaxing hobbies

Whenever I have extra time and I want to get away and relax I like to go fishing. Sometimes I take the kids with me, but most of the time I go to get some quiet time and to collect my thoughts. I like to travel all over the central valley to find good fishing spots. This year I haven't really had any luck finding the bite, but either way I enjoy the time away and the peace and quiet. I plan on doing more fishing with the kids in the future. Especially Hailee, she loves to go fishing with daddy.
